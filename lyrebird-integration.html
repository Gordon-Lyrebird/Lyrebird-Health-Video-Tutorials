How to Integrate Lyrebird into Your Best Practice Database

Step-by-Step Instructions

Overview: This guide will walk you through the process of integrating Lyrebird Health into your Best Practice database. Once integrated, clinicians will be able to access Lyrebird directly from patient records to streamline documentation and clinical workflows.
⚠️ Important: The initial integration of Lyrebird as a third-party software must be completed by a user with administrative rights to the Best Practice database. Once the integration is set up by an administrator, all clinicians can then create their individual accounts and access Lyrebird.
  1. In Best Practice, navigate to Setup from the main menu.
  2. Go to Configurations, then select Database.
  3. Setup third-party integrations.
  4. Search for Lyrebird Health in the integrations list.
  5. Once you've located Lyrebird Health, complete the integration by clicking Save.
  6. To verify the integration was successful, open any patient's record in Best Practice.
  7. Look for the L icon at the very top of the patient record. This icon indicates that Lyrebird has been successfully integrated into your database.
  8. When a clinician clicks the L icon for the first time, they will be prompted to create their Lyrebird account.
  9. After the clinician completes the account creation process, their account will be successfully integrated with Best Practice, and they can begin using Lyrebird for clinical documentation.
Verification Checklist:
  • ✓ Lyrebird Health appears in 3rd Party Integrations
  • ✓ Integration has been saved
  • ✓ L icon is visible at the top of patient records
  • ✓ Clinicians can click the L icon and create accounts
Pro Tip: Have clinicians create their accounts before seeing patients to ensure a smooth workflow. Each clinician only needs to create their account once, and it will remain linked to the Best Practice database.
What Happens After Integration:
  • Clinicians can access Lyrebird directly from any patient record
  • Patient data from Best Practice automatically syncs with Lyrebird
  • Clinical documentation created in Lyrebird saves back to Best Practice
  • All clinicians in the practice can use Lyrebird once they've created their individual accounts
